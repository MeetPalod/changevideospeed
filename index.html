changevideospeed

Adjust video playback speed while streaming online on various platforms beyond permissible limit. Tested on Youtube, Amazon Prime Video, Netflix, Hotstar.

Usage Instructions

Only works with HTML5 video players.
1. Ensure that your browser's bookmarks bar is visible. (Ctrl+Shift+B for chrome)
2. Enter the desired speed of the speed up in the corresponding input field.
3. Drag the resulting button below the text field and drop it in the bookmarks bar of browser.
4. Press the button in the bookmarks bar when the video is open to get the desired playback speed.
5. If you press the button again, the playback speed will be back to original.
